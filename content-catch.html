Bad Content Isn’t Worth Decorating

Content is the most overlooked design problem. Tight budgets get eaten up by other needs, like visual design, since it’s harder to look away from and feels more urgent.

Failed content leads to loss of sales, loss of attention, and suspicion. When this happens, the chance for genuine connection is gone. You could be damaging your reputation or product by attempting content that is big on fluff and short on facts.

Plus, writing for yourself is hard without someone there to bounce ideas off or do a tone check.
